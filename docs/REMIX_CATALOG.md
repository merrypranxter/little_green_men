**Desc**: Just the head. Clean. SVG + canvas versions.

**Desc**: Full body, animated dance loop. Arms out, hip sway, the whole nine.

**Desc**: Head only, but the eyes are doing something wrong. Pupil dilation, chromatic aberration, color cycling.

**Desc**: Classic saucer with beam of light. Alien descending/ascending in beam.
**Files**: `sketches/p5js/alien_ufo.js`

**Desc**: Hard pixel grid, limited palette, Space Invaders energy.

### 7. Psychedelic Shader — `alien_psychedelic.frag`
**Desc**: Domain warping on the alien form, color cycling, fractal background.
